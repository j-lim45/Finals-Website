Admissions

University of Hallownest is now accepting new applicants for Batch 2026! Please read the instructions below to be guided on the process for admissions into UH.

How to Apply

1. Become eligible for admission which requires being a first-year college applicant who has not enrolled in any other university or has not taken any college units.

2. Provide documents for admission and subsequent enrollment if accepted: • Grade 12 Copy of Grades • Letter of Recommendation • Birth Certificate • Medical Certificate • Social Security Number • Curriculum Vitae • Valid ID • Police Clearance • NBI Clearance

3. Submit an admission form in person or online through the Apply button below.

4. Pass the entrance exam to be successfully admitted into the university.
