Dean Thistlewind speaks before Graduating Batch of 2024

Author: Cloth | Written: Sep 4 2024

During the commencement exercise of the Batch of 2024 last Aug. 25, our esteemed Dean Thistlewind was the resource speaker and delivered an inspiring speech for the graduating students who will move on as the new professionals of Hallownest.

In her speech, she gave praise to those individuals who gave utmost dedication in attaining their diplomas. She noted "It is not only the pieces of paper that you work hard for but the nourishment of yourselves and for Hallownest." She also thanked the faculty and staff for uplifting the students who were with them from Day 1. A notable highlight from the speech was the presentation of a short video clip taken from ThouTube about short inspirational quotes for graduating students. The attendees gave a standing ovation as the clip ended, showing their enthusiasm for the Dean.

Thistlewind, Ph.D took over as Dean of the College of Engineering and Technology last 2021 after the resignation of Hegemol. She graduated from the University of Hallownest with a Bachelor's in Education, and took her Master's at the Celestial University in Vancouver.
