College of the Arts and Science

Objective

In line with Monomon's teachings, the College of the Arts and Science seeks to shape artistic and humanistic individuals with the intent of developing the culture of Hallownest. Its mission is focused on communicating creative ideas, and learning the answer within what makes Hallownest.
