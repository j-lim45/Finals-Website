College of Engineering and Technology

Objective

Formed by the principles of the Pale King, the College of Engineering and Technology strives to equip bugs with skills that are needed in the technological advancement of Hallownest. Through the college, it seeks to nourish bugs that train them to become highly competent professionals in the field while being sensitive individuals to the conservation of Hallownest.
