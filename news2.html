UH among top universities in new WS rankings

Author: Cornifer | Written: Aug 27 2024

The University of Hallownest continues to be one of the premier universities in a new editiion of the WS university rankings released yesterday.

According to the list, The University of Hallownest garnered the Top 2 spot in the rankings behind Stardew University. Other inclusions in the list were the University of Pharloom, Celestial University, and the Institute of Blackroot. The criterion of the ranking is based on numerous factors such as the number of citations, student-to-faculty ratio, student satisfaction, and quality of life. UH soared in the ranking for citations, accounting for more than twice the second place university.

Dean Monomon took note of the newly published ranking and applauded the students and faculty of UH. "This is a welcoming news from the publication and this only solidifies the university's mission to develop individuals through quality and holistic education, and this was demonstrated through our faculty and students who help keep us as one of the premier institutions in the world." Since the publication's conception in 1973, the University of Hallownest has consistently stayed in the Top 5 universities in the world.
